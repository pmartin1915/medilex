# Privacy Policy for Healthcare Vocabulary App

**Last Updated:** November 17, 2025

## Overview

Healthcare Vocabulary App ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
The App does NOT collect, transmit, or store any personally identifiable information (PII). All data is stored locally on your device.

### Data Stored Locally
The App stores the following data locally on your device:
- **Study Progress**: Your learning progress for medical terms
- **Favorites**: Medical terms you've marked as favorites
- **Bookmarks**: Terms you've bookmarked for later review
- **Study Streak**: Your daily study streak and history
- **Settings**: Your app preferences and configurations
- **Error Logs**: Technical logs for debugging (stored locally only)

### Information We DO NOT Collect
- We do NOT collect your name, email, or contact information
- We do NOT collect location data
- We do NOT collect device identifiers for tracking
- We do NOT transmit your study data to external servers
- We do NOT use analytics or tracking services
- We do NOT share data with third parties

## How We Use Your Information

All data is used solely to provide you with a personalized learning experience:
- Track your study progress and mastery levels
- Calculate and display your study streaks
- Remember your favorite and bookmarked terms
- Provide personalized statistics and achievements
- Debug technical issues (logs stored locally only)

## Data Storage & Security

### Local Storage
All data is stored locally on your device using secure storage mechanisms provided by your operating system (iOS/Android). We do not have access to this data.

### Data Retention
Your data persists on your device until:
- You uninstall the App
- You manually clear the App's data through system settings
- You use the in-app "Clear All Data" feature

### No Cloud Backup
We do NOT back up your data to cloud services. If you uninstall the App or reset your device, your progress will be lost.

## Third-Party Services

The App does NOT use any third-party services for:
- Analytics
- Advertising
- Crash reporting
- User tracking
- Data collection

The App uses only the following system-level services:
- **Local Storage** (AsyncStorage): For saving your progress
- **Text-to-Speech** (Optional): For pronouncing medical terms (requires speech synthesis permission)
- **Haptic Feedback** (Optional): For touch feedback

## Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The App is designed for:
- Medical students
- Healthcare professionals
- Adults learning medical terminology

If you are under 13, please do not use this App without parental supervision.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access Your Data**: View all your data within the App's Debug screen
- **Delete Your Data**: Use the "Clear All Data" feature in Settings
- **Export Your Data**: Currently not supported, but all data is visible in the App

## Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete all App data by:

1. **iOS**:
   - Settings → General → iPhone Storage → Healthcare Vocabulary App → Delete App

2. **Android**:
   - Settings → Apps → Healthcare Vocabulary App → Storage → Clear Data

3. **In-App**:
   - Open App → Settings → Advanced → Clear All Data
